Polite Politics

A private group of people all across the American political spectrum engaging in meaningful discourse

Rules:

  • No name calling that's an ad hominem and it's not helpful.
  • Please attempt to state your opinions as opinions and not facts. This is key in staving off unnecessary arguments.
  • Mind your language. While we are adults here please refrain from overuse of curse words.
  • We probably won't solve all the world's or nation's problems but we can take this serious...but not too serious.
  • I reserve the right to boot people if they break these rules repeatedly.

Suggestions for helpful discourse:

  • When posting articles please make sure you've actually read the article.
  • The best conversation starters are typically questions.
  • Seek to understand someone's position before commenting on it. It's okay to ask clarifying questions.
  • Avoid assumptions about others, especially based on their perceived political position.
